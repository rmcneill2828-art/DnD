## Personality
- **Traits:** Measured, resigned rather than defiant. Cooperates fully now that the bargain he inherited has been broken.
- **Ideals:** Confirmed, in the end, to believe the office he held could still be redeemed even if he personally couldn't — revealed Merrick as his groomed successor as a final act of contrition.
- **Bonds:** Whatever loyalty he once owed "the Warden's" office, it broke the moment the anchor did.
- **Flaws:** Spent years quietly grooming Merrick as a successor without ever telling him — a last, unresolved piece of the harm he caused.

## Backstory
Edmund Vale is the Warden — not a deeper hidden figure behind a title, but the title's actual current holder, inherited rather than personal (his signet ring, worn smooth from more than one owner's hand, confirms as much). Captured alive during the mine's fall, he ultimately confirmed the full truth: the coded ledgers were a partial copy of the bargain's own contract, and he himself was the last piece the party needed to understand Duskgate. His final act was revealing that he'd been quietly grooming Merrick as his successor — without Merrick's knowledge — a confession that closed the campaign's central mystery.
