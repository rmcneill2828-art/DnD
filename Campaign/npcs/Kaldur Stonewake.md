## Personality
- **Traits:** Steady, plainspoken, quietly proud of what Urskelde has become. Trades old hunting stories and Goliath culture freely with anyone willing to listen.
- **Ideals:** Loyalty earned, not owed. He was a prisoner himself before Darkhawk found him — he doesn't forget what that cost, or who paid it.
- **Bonds:** Calls Darkhawk "brother," not just an ally — a bond both have said plainly and meant. Feels a standing duty to guide and protect Urskelde's growing settlement.
- **Flaws:** Downplays his own exile from Ninebells (his birth clan) rather than dealing with it directly.

## Backstory
Found by Darkhawk as a long-held prisoner during the Circuit's dismantling — an older Goliath who recognized Darkhawk's carried trophy as Thundertree clan craftsmanship, giving Darkhawk his first real lead on his own identity. Exiled from his own clan (Ninebells) for reasons not yet told in full, he chose to stay and help rather than return to a home that no longer wanted him. Guided Darkhawk and Mara into the unmapped Greyhorn passes toward Thundertree, and has run Urskelde's garrison — training deputies Idra and Torvin, holding the base secure through the Duskgate strike and its aftermath — ever since.
