- **Cunning Action:** on each of his turns, he can use a bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.
- **Sneak Attack (1/turn):** deals an extra 2d6 damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 ft of an ally of his that isn't incapacitated and he doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

## Personality
- **Traits:** Observant, quick-witted, more capable than his quiet administrative role ever let on — Vale saw something in him worth grooming, even if Merrick himself never knew why.
- **Ideals:** Untested — he's never been told what he was actually being prepared for.
- **Bonds:** Unknowing loyalty to Vale, whatever that's worth once the full truth reaches him.
- **Flaws:** Genuinely unaware he was Vale's chosen successor to the Warden's bargain — a secret that could land very differently depending on how and when he learns it.

## Backstory
Vale quietly groomed Merrick as his successor to the Warden's bargain without ever telling him what that meant — a fact only confirmed after Vale's capture. Merrick himself has no idea. He's currently held at Urskelde alongside Vale, unaware of the role he was being prepared for or the danger his own presence still represents (per the succession mechanic: the bargain has no one to renew into as long as neither he nor Vale reaches Duskgate again).
